Privacy Policy

BizAgent is an app for invoicing and expense tracking. We respect your privacy.

Data we process

  • Accounting data you enter (invoices, expenses, business settings).
  • Account login data (Firebase Authentication).
  • Technical identifiers required to operate the service (e.g., user ID in the database).

Where data is stored

Your data is stored in Firebase/Firestore under your user account. Access is limited to authenticated users.

Camera & files

  • Camera may be used to scan receipts (OCR) to help create expenses.
  • The app may generate and share exports (e.g., PDF/ZIP) via the system share dialog.

Sharing with third parties

We do not sell your data. Data may be processed by Google Firebase services (hosting, database, authentication) to provide the app functionality.

Security

We use authentication and database access rules. We recommend using strong sign-in and not sharing your account.

Contact

If you have privacy questions, contact: support@bizagent.sk
